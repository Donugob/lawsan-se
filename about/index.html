Our Foundational Principles

To Foster Unity, Intellectualism, and Professionalism.

The Law Students' Association of Nigeria (LAWSAN), South East Zone, is mandated to create a seamless bond among all law faculties in the region. We are committed to organizing world-class academic and social programs that enhance the learning experience, promote healthy competition, and prepare our members for the rigors and prestige of the legal profession.

Our history is rich with impactful leadership and landmark events that have shaped the legal landscape for students in the South East.
